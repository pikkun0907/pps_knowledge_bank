## Convex Opt problem

:::theory

$P$: 
$$

\text{minimize} ~~ f_0(x) \\
s.t. ~~ f_i(x)\le 0 , ~~Ax=b

$$

:::

Equivalent expression:

:::theory
$P'$: 
$$

\text{minimize} ~~ t\\
s.t. ~~ f_0(x)\le t,~~f_i(x)\le 0 , ~~Ax=b

$$
:::

$P \iff P'$. 

$f_0(x)$ and $f_i(x)$ are convex. 

<mark>The feasible set of a convex optimization problem is convex.</mark>
